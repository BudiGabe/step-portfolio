Personal Projects

Dungeon Clicker

Developed when clicker games were gaining a lot of traction, in this game you are a hero who must go through multiple levels in a dungeon where monsters get tougher and tougher. Slay enemies, level up, upgrade abilities and see how far you can go.

Workout Planner

When you're working out, calculating the weight of your next set is tedious. With this app, just insert the percentage you want to work with and let it do the work for you. Moreover, you can use the in-built templates for the 5/3/1 workout and it's brother, BBB.

Kitesurfing App

I didn't even know that kitesurfing is a thing before making this app. One of the more refined apps from this list, you can find kitesurfing spots all around the world, along with useful information such as wind speed. And you can favorite spots so you never miss them.

Mini Overlord(Ongoing Project)

Good games are hard to come by, so why not make your own? Take the role of an evil overlord and defend your base from the goody two-shoes heroes.
